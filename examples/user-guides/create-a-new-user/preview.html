Adding a new user and putting them in a team

New colleagues need a Cronmon account before they can see their team's jobs. This guide walks through creating the account and adding it to a team. You'll need admin rights — if you can't see Admin in the sidebar, ask an existing administrator.

1Log in

Go to Cronmon and sign in with your usual username and password.

2Open the admin area

Click Admin in the sidebar, then choose Users.

3Create the user

Click New user. Fill in the person's SSO username, their forenames and surname, and their email address, then click Save.

The new account is a normal user — if they need admin rights, use the Admin toggle next to their name in the users list afterwards.

4Find the team

Head back to Admin in the sidebar and choose Teams, then click the team the person belongs to — here that's Application Development.

5Add them to the team

Click the Add a user… box and type part of the person's name to narrow the list. Pick them, then click Add. They'll appear in the members list straight away, and they'll see the team's jobs the next time they log in.
